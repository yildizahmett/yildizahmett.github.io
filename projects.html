Spine Posture Detection Using Machine Learning

My friend and I embarked on an innovative project where we worked together to design a system that employs advanced machine learning algorithms to identify poor spine posture. Our main goal was to develop a reliable and precise system capable of recognizing misalignments in the spine, with the aim of enhancing posture awareness and potentially preventing associated health issues. The resulting system has great potential for raising awareness about spinal health, particularly in situations where individuals may not have immediate access to healthcare professionals. By accurately identifying instances of bad spine posture, the system can promptly provide feedback to users, motivating them to improve their posture and adopt healthier habits. This collaborative project successfully created a system that utilizes advanced machine learning methods to detect improper spine posture. The system's ability to accurately classify spine postures represents a significant advancement in the promotion of better posture and awareness of spinal health. It serves as a testament to the positive impact of technology on public health and underscores the importance of interdisciplinary cooperation in addressing real-world health-related challenges.

MEF MOOC

This website is specifically designed to address the challenge of students selecting online course bundles to fulfill their elective course requirements. Students have the capability to assemble bundles, which instructors can review and either endorse or decline. This project is developed for MF University as part of a class assignment, and it utilizes Flask and PostgreSQL for its implementation.

UP School

A platform resembling LinkedIn, where students and alumni can input their details to showcase themselves to prospective employers in their job search. Companies have the ability to sort through student profiles to identify the talents they need. This website was constructed using Flask and PostgreSQL.

Multimodal Fake News Detection

In this project, I created a fake news detection system that utilizes both textual and visual elements from news articles, embracing a multimodal approach. The project highlighted the critical role of multimodal data analysis and the importance of feature engineering in the realm of machine learning. It underscored the significance of feature engineering in the context of machine learning, particularly when dealing with multimodal data analysis. The project also exemplified the importance of multimodal data analysis, harnessing both textual and visual attributes to enhance overall performance. Valuable insights were gained from the project regarding the trade-offs between various feature engineering techniques and the significance of multimodal data analysis, all of which contribute to the advancement of machine learning and data science.
